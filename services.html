Services

Five services, one team.

Whether you're moving a single LCL pallet or chartering an oversized project lift, the coordinator on your account stays the same.

Ocean · FCL & LCL

Weekly sailings on every main lane.

Reliable FCL and LCL service across the trans-Pacific, Asia–Europe, intra-Asia, transatlantic and Indian-Ocean corridors. Direct-carrier contracts and a NVOCC desk that knows when to use which.

  • Rate transparency: line, surcharges, BAF/GRI all itemised.
  • Proactive blank-sailing alerts — we re-route before you ask.
  • Reefer, OOG, breakbulk and HAZ-class handling on the same B/L.
  • LCL CFS network across 24 gateway cities, weekly schedules.

Air freight

Consol, express, charter — your call.

Consolidated and direct air capacity on 60+ daily lanes with same-week confirmation. SAF-blend options on partner carriers where they're available, charter desk on standby when your cargo needs its own aircraft.

  • Same-day pickup on most lanes — AWB live within 4 hours.
  • Temperature-controlled ULDs for pharma and perishables (CEIV).
  • Hand-carrier and on-board courier desk for critical spares.
  • SAF blend reporting integrated with your sustainability ledger.

Customs brokerage

Cleared in hours, not days.

Licensed brokers in 40+ jurisdictions handle classification, valuation, FTA qualification, ACE/EAD/ENS filings, AEO and CTPAT compliance — usually within hours of arrival.

  • Pre-clearance filed before the vessel berths.
  • Bond posting and duty deferment on every gateway.
  • Free-trade agreement screening — we find the duty savings.
  • Audit trails ready for AEO, CTPAT, OEA inspections.

Warehousing & 3PL

Bonded, FTZ-capable, WMS-ready.

Six strategic gateways with bonded and FTZ facilities for pick-pack, kitting, returns and B2C fulfilment. Real-time WMS integration via API or EDI, daily SLA reporting baked in.

  • 280,000 sqm bonded + 120,000 sqm B2C-grade pick-pack.
  • Same-day cut-off for next-business-day domestic.
  • Returns & refurb pipeline integrated with your ERP.
  • Pharma-grade temperature zones in 4 gateway facilities.

Project cargo

Heavy-lift & turnkey moves.

Route surveys, escort coordination, multimodal sequencing, on-site supervision. From a 380-tonne transformer to a wind-blade convoy — we engineer the lane before we book it.

  • Method statements, lift plans, route surveys included.
  • Chartered breakbulk, RoRo, semi-submersibles, AN-class freighters.
  • Permit coordination across 60+ countries.
  • On-site superintendents for critical lifts.

Cargo insurance & trade finance

Quoted alongside every booking.

Door-to-door all-risk cover via underwritten partners, plus partner-backed trade finance — quoted alongside every booking so nothing slows down. Claims handled by Sunbridge, no third-party run-around.

  • Open cover for repeat shippers — one policy, all lanes.
